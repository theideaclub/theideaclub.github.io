About Us

The Idea Club is the brainchild of Claude and Casper, two San Francisco Bay Area entrepreneurs who felt like they had so many great product concepts that they didn't know what to do with it. The idea (pun intended) was to create a space for the collaboration and sharing of product ideas with businesses and entrepeneurs. And it's all free - we wanted to replace dollars with smiles and patents with handshakes. Remember, there's no such thing as a bad idea. We can work with anything.
